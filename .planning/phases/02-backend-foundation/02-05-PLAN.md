| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-02-14 | Tampering (integrity) | Double-effect: at-least-once redelivery applies a side-effect twice (replay / double-spend class) | mitigate | Mandatory idempotent listeners (D-11, natural-key check-before-write); `KillListenerTest.sideEffectAppliedExactlyOnceAfterRedelivery` proves exactly-once effect |
| T-02-15 | Denial of service | Infinite retry of a poison event saturating the registry/scheduler | mitigate | Bounded retry (D-12) capped at `maxAttempts`; past the bound the publication stays INCOMPLETE + metric + log; `BoundedRetryTest` proves the cap |
| T-02-16 | Repudiation / availability | Silent event drop hides a failed side-effect (no audit, no alert) | mitigate | No silent drop: exhausted retries leave the record INCOMPLETE, emit a log line, and increment `EventPublicationMetrics` (alertable); staleness monitor flags stuck publications |
